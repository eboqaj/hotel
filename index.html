About Hotel Centrum

Hotel Centrum blends boutique character with contemporary comfort in the very center of the city, creating a calm and elevated stay for business trips, weekend escapes, and long-awaited celebrations.

For more than two decades, we have welcomed guests with warm service, refined interiors, and thoughtful details that make every arrival feel effortless. From sunrise breakfasts to late-night concierge care, every moment is designed to feel personal.
